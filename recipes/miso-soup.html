Miso soup

A Japanese soup made from a dashi stock and flavored with miso paste.

Usually contains tofu and green onions. It can also feature seaweed, mushrooms, daikon, and more.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dashi granules
  • Miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Step 1:the water and dashi granules to a boil.
  2. Step 2:Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Step 3:Stir in the tofu and green onions.
  4. Step 4:Simmer and serve.
